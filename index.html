About Me

I'm a graduate of the Masters program in computer science at the University of Nevada, Reno (UNR). For my undergraduate degree, I completed a double major in computer science and mathematics at UNR. My research focused on genetic algorithms and discrete optimization for planning routes for robotic vehicles to conduct automated bridge inspection. I'm passionate about software development, genetic algorithms, machine learning, distributed and parallel computing, and all things AI. Here you can find links to my research, and many samples of my coding projects that demonstrate my passion and experience in these areas.
